# NoRoadDown

**Welcome to NO ROAD DOWN**

**High Concept**: No Road Down is a single-player, 3rd-person self-racing game where you play as a kart and try to escape the thrilled and smashing boulder. You play as a kart being chased by a huge boulder hurtling down a mountain, eager to smash it. The player will progress through three levels, with the difficulty increasing with each level. The player doesn't just have the boulder as an obstacle; there are also environmental obstacles to avoid and more boulders that try to stop him from escaping the great boulder. 

**Controls**

- Moving Right and Left: A, D.
- Using Boost: Shift

**Debug Controls**

- To Open Debug Controls: 0
- Increase Kart Speed: I
- Decrease Kart Speed: O
- Increase Boulder Speed: N
- Decrease Boulder Speed: M
- Clear player's saved Settings: -
- Have Boost: B
- Move to Level 1: 1
- Move to Level 2: 2
- Move to Level 3: 3
